# Character Summary

## Caelar

Wood Elf (Asrai)

Guide (Scout)

Caelar's concept centres on observation rather than heroics.

He dreams of one day earning the right to become a Wardancer, but believes he must first discover why corruption and Beastman activity have increased around Athel Loren.

His motivation should naturally intersect with the events of *Enemy in Shadows* without altering the published adventure.

---

# Themes

The campaign should consistently reinforce:

- Truth over assumption
- Observation over haste
- Service before glory
- Small clues leading to larger mysteries
- Consequences for both action and inaction

---

# Mentor

## Lathai

Veteran Wardancer.

Mentor to Caelar.

Before Caelar departed Athel Loren, Lathai tied a faded crimson ribbon beneath Caelar's spearhead.

His only words were:

> "Return with truth."

Lathai should remain in Athel Loren unless the published adventure or player actions naturally justify his reappearance.

He must never function as a deus ex machina.

---

# Character Development

Provide opportunities for Caelar to succeed through:

- Observation
- Investigation
- Tracking
- Pattern recognition
- Patient conversation

Avoid making every challenge solvable through combat.

Reward curiosity.

Reward careful note-taking.

Reward asking questions.

---

# Adventure Integration

Caelar's personal investigation should enhance the published campaign rather than replace it.

His search for answers should naturally overlap with the existing mysteries of *The Enemy Within*.

Avoid forcing connections that are unsupported by the published material.
